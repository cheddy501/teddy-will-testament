POA for Healthcare & Personal Care

We prepare Powers of Attorney for healthcare and personal care, allowing you to appoint a trusted individual to make medical and personal care decisions on your behalf if you are unable to do so yourself. Our team ensures the document is drafted clearly and accurately, providing you peace of mind that your healthcare wishes and personal care will be handled according to your preferences.

What is a POA for Healthcare?

A Power of Attorney for Healthcare & Personal Care is a legal document that authorizes someone to act on your behalf regarding medical decisions, treatment plans, and personal care choices if you become incapacitated or unable to communicate your wishes. Your appointed representative can speak with doctors, manage care plans, and ensure decisions align with your values and beliefs during critical times.

Our experienced team guides you through preparing this important document in compliance with Belize’s legal requirements, ensuring your healthcare and personal care decisions are honored and your loved ones are supported during difficult times.
